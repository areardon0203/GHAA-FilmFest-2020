Today's Featured Films

Today we are pleased to release our first group of student and staff entries into the GHAA Filmfest 2020! Thank you to everyone who helped and participated, we are all so proud of the work everyone create!

The New Generation - Tobey Eckert

This film was made to showcase where modern climbers train



Being American

This brief documentary was created by the students in the class Race, Gender and Identity and highlights their ideas about what it means to be "American" in 2020. Written and Produced by: Makayla Watkins, Violet McCabe, Rhiannon Goulet, Kasie Leander, Celeste Bryant



Stillness - Kiara Burgos

This project: Music/Spoken Word Video was for Movement Rep. The objective was to create a video focusing on topic that means a lot to you while showing physical expressions to tell the story.



Look Out - Maya Hennessey

A young woman struggles to get peaceful sleep as a mysterious creature from her sleep haunts and controls her with fear. Cast:  Young Woman: Cory McCullough  Demon: Juliana Goncalves  Crew:  Director/Editor/Writer: Maya Hennessey Director of Photography/Assistant Director: Eddie Chavez Sound Mixer/Boom/Producer: Davinus Onuoha Made in association with the University of Southern California



Confined Spaces - Skylar Longley

This movement piece was created under the heading of Quartantine Theater. The use of a confined space, a familiar object, and repetition was incorporated to highlight the theme of dark verses light.



Time is Running Out - Leandra Trotman

This music video was created for a project for Movement Rep based on the use of a confined space, repetition and familiar objects.



Who We Are - Julian Cucuta

Based on a photography research project



Pandemic Journal Entry 2,3,5 - Nancy Doherty

Video journal entries of a singular day in the pandemic, where all days seem to run into one, and based on the given prompt, Time and Distance.



A Race for Time and Distance - Petr Zierlein

A car race is primarily about time and distance. This film shows a racer doing his thing, trying to make the distance. A study in speed and elegance. Cast: Peter Zierlein Camera: Nathan Zierlein



Clouds of Quarantine - Russel Ford

Timelapse of clouds and sunsets
